About SurfaceScore

The leading compatibility testing tool for Safari Reader Mode and Apple Intelligence optimization.

Our Mission

SurfaceScore was created to help developers optimize their websites for Apple's ecosystem. As Safari Reader Mode and Apple Intelligence become increasingly important for content consumption, we provide the tools you need to ensure your content surfaces beautifully across Apple's platforms.

What We Do

We analyze your website's compatibility with:

  • Safari Reader Mode - Ensure your content displays cleanly in distraction-free reading mode
  • Apple Intelligence - Optimize for AI summarization and content understanding
  • WCAG 2.1 Compliance - Meet accessibility standards for all users
  • Structured Data - Validate Schema.org markup for better content understanding

Why It Matters

With millions of Safari users relying on Reader Mode for distraction-free browsing and Apple Intelligence transforming how content is consumed, compatibility testing is essential. SurfaceScore helps you:

  • Improve content visibility in Apple's ecosystem
  • Enhance user experience across Safari and iOS
  • Meet modern accessibility standards
  • Optimize for AI-powered content summarization

Technology

SurfaceScore is built with privacy and performance in mind:

  • All analysis runs locally in your browser
  • No personal data collection or storage
  • Fast, real-time compatibility checking
  • Industry-standard accessibility algorithms
