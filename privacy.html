Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

SurfaceScore is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our Safari Reader Mode and Apple Intelligence compatibility checker.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data. SurfaceScore operates entirely in your browser:

  • Website URLs you analyze are processed locally
  • Analysis results are generated in your browser
  • No personal information is transmitted to our servers
  • No tracking cookies are used

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how our tool is used:

  • Anonymous usage statistics only
  • No personally identifiable information
  • You can opt out using browser settings

Third-Party Services

SurfaceScore may fetch publicly available web pages for analysis. This is done directly from your browser to the target website - we don't act as an intermediary.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@surfacescore.com
